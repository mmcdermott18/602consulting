Meet Tim McDermott

Tim McDermott is an experienced fundraiser with nearly 40 years of professional development experience. He has served public and private institutions of higher education, academic medical centers, and non-profits. He has ten years of consulting experience serving over 24 non-profit organizations. His fundraising expertise includes service to Feed More, Virginia Wesleyan University, Warren Alpert Medical School of Brown University, Virginia Commonwealth University School of Medicine, Duke University, and Virginia Tech. At Feed More, Central Virginia’s core hunger-relief organization, Tim directed their successful $19.5 million comprehensive campaign. He received his Master of Public Administration degree from Virginia Commonwealth University and his Bachelor of Arts degree in Geography from Virginia Tech. He is a member of the VCU Health Board of Directors and serves as Chair of the Board for Downtown Ashland Association.

Services

Campaign Design and Implementation

Are you ready for a campaign? We’ll ensure you are, from Development Office assessments, volunteer readiness, feasibility studies, budget review to campaign volunteer recruitment, training, and ongoing campaign management, we will help ensure you put prospects and donors first so that the hard work of a campaign helps you accomplish your mission.

Show me the Gifts

Donors are looking for a relationship. Sometimes that is from a distance, other times it requires a deep personal relationship with staff, volunteers, and an organization’s mission. Finding the correct path to his relationship and stewarding it through time is essential and one of the hallmarks of our work.

Board Members or Bored Members

Engaged volunteer leadership is essential to fundraising success, today and tomorrow. We help develop strategies to keep current board members involved while focusing on the future through robust succession planning.

Planning and Strategy

We will help develop donor-focused plans for all fundraising and related activity.
